Sales & Profit Dashboard (Excel + Power BI)

A multi-company analytical dashboard with full time-series tracking.

This project combines Excel and Power BI to analyse sales, costs, and profitability across multiple companies and product categories over time.
It includes a dynamic calendar table (Day, Month, Quarter, Year) to support time-based insights.

 WHAT THE PROJECT DOES

Consolidates raw datasets containing:

Company

Product

COGM (Cost of Goods Manufactured)

COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)

Revenue

Profit + Profit Margin

Calendar date tracking (Day, Month, Year)

Cleans and structures the data using Excel formulas and Power Query.

Builds a connected date table for proper time intelligence in Power BI.

Creates DAX measures for sales, cost, profit, and trends.

-DASHBOARD AND VISUAL INSIGHTS-

Includes:

Sales & Profit Overview (KPIs + bar charts)

COGM vs COGS analysis

Profit margin by company & product

Time-series trends (daily, monthly, quarterly, yearly)

Date slicers for flexible filtering

Seasonality patterns (peak sales months, performance cycles)

-SKILLS DEMONSTRATED-

Data cleaning & transformation (Excel, Power Query)

Building Power BI data models with relationship between fact table and date table (Calendar)

KPI creation (Total Sales, Gross Profit)

Time-based analysis (YoY, MoM, daily trends)

Dashboard design and storytelling

-HOW TO USE-

Review the raw and cleaned Excel data (data transformation made on PowerBi in screenshots folder).

Open the Power BI file and explore:

Company slicer

Month/Year slicer

Product category slicer

Check how performance changes over time and compare companies or products.
